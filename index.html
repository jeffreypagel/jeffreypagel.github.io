Welcome! I am a Fellow in Environmental Economics at the London School of Economics. My research lies at the intersection of development, environmental, and resource economics, with an emphasis on public policy. I use geospatial impact evaluations to understand the human and ecological impacts of development programs and public policies.

Additionally, I work as an Ethical and Project Evaluation Consultant. Previously, I was a Research Officer at the Grantham Research Institute at the London School of Economics. I received my PhD from the University of Barcelona in 2021.
